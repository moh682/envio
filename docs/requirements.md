## Invoice

### Fields

- Invoice number required
- Issue date required
- Is Vat
- One Of required
  - Name
  - Email
  - Phone
  - Car registration
- Products required
  - Id optional
  - Description required
  - Quantity required
  - Amount required
  - Total required must be with 2 decimals
